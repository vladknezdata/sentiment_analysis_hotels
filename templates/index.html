Sentiment analysis overview

SENTIMENT ANALYSIS is a powerful tool for business owners/operators to analyze social emotions and sentiments related to their particular business, product, services, amenities or events to determine the effects of these sentiments. These effects help the operators gain more market share, remain competitive in the market place, enhance their services/products/capabilities, charge premium pricing where possible, offer discounts to attract new business, and inspire myriad of other decisions as applicable.

Focus: Use varied Machine Learning Models to assess Sentiment Analysis of Hotels using Tripadvisor.com reviews/content

STEPS 1. Web-crawl and scrape TripAdvisor.com for hotel name, location, customer review score, customer review text into HTML files 2. Parse all HTML files for a location into one CSV 3. Upload the data into Mongo DB cluster (collection and documents) 4. SENTIMENT ANALYSIS: 4a. Data Munging - Arduous but necessary for each location! 4b. Generate VADER Score: Valence Aware Dictionary for sEntiment Reasoning is a model used for text sentiment analysis that is sensitive to both polarity (positive/negative) and intensity (strength) of emotion 4c. Word Frequency: Top 10 positive and negative words, Top 10 bi-grams positive and negative words 4d. Mutual Information - In probability theory and information theory, the mutual information (MI) of two random variables is a measure of the mutual dependence between the two variables 4e. Pointwise Mutual Information - PMI has been used for finding collocations and associations between words. For instance, countings of occurrences and co-occurrences of words in a text corpus can be used to approximate the probabilities p(x) and p(x,y) respectively
